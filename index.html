Welcome to the Smart Canteen Demand Predictor. Designed to help canteen managers effortlessly calculate how many meals to prepare, reducing both food waste and shortages.

The Problem

Canteens often struggle to know exactly how much food to cook. Cooking too much leads to food waste, while cooking too little leads to food shortages and unhappy customers.

The Solution

Our intelligent system uses a rule-based algorithm considering the day, meal time, weather, and special events to provide a reliable estimation of food demand.

The Benefits

  • Save money on ingredients
  • Protect the environment
  • Ensure everyone gets a meal

About Us

Smart Canteen Demand Predictor was created with a singular mission: to make canteen management sustainable, easy, and stress-free.

Every year, thousands of tons of perfectly good food go to waste simply because it's difficult to predict how many people will eat at the canteen on a given day. Weather, day of the week, and special events all play a part.

Our goal is to help modern and traditional canteens take the guesswork out of their kitchen. This tool was designed specifically so that anyone, regardless of technical skill, can open the website, enter a few details, and get a reliable recommendation.

Our Vision

Zero food waste across local communities.
